Objective

This case study is about a global manufacturer of bio-based specialty chemicals called GreenFuture Biochem with five core markets, encompassing 50 brands and over 750 SKUs over the world. The goal is to analyze the company performance on Sales, R&D, Manufacturing, and Supply Chain, proposing a holistic view of its profitability and sustainability and identifying areas for improvement.

Methodology

  • The provided datasets were mostly clean, so we mainly used Excel for data exploration, cleaning, and dataset joining.
  • Tableau served as a main tool for visualizations and building three dashboards.

Results

  • Short-term strategy (within a year)
    • Optimize Supplier Mix: lower 10% of order quantities from dominant suppliers using the CO2 avoided per $ of procurement shift metric.
    • Refocus sales efforts on key categories/segments: prioritize late-stage opportunities with higher probability in Industrial Lubricants & Automotive Solutions, and refine value messaging to increase win-rate in Consumer & Home Care.
  • Long-term strategy (1â€“3 years)
    • Supply diversification: source materials closer to production plants to reduce logistics costs and emissions.
    • R&D acceleration: invest in Specialty Polymers and Automotive Solutions with high sales potential to innovate more cost-efficient, high-margin products.
    • Green procurement KPIs: adopt sustainable KPIs (e.g., CO2 per $1M purchased; Supplier Sustainable Index weighted by unit cost, emissions, and on-time delivery scores).
    • Real-time Performance Tracking: use dashboards to monitor key sustainability and profitability metrics continuously.

Objective

Build an end-to-end pipeline that extracts text from Deltaâ€™s corporate PDFs, retrieves relevant evidence, and answers questions strictly from verified sources in a user-friendly interface.

Methodology

  • Document Collection: Delta Air Lines 2024 Form 10-K, 2024 ESG Report, and major shareholders summary from Yahoo Finance.
  • Data Processing Pipeline: PDF extraction with pdfplumber; OCR-first for the scan-like ESG report; cleaning, standardization, and sliding-window chunking (~600 words with 100-word overlap) into a unified store.
  • Retrieval System: TF-IDF vectorization over all chunks with cosine similarity to rank top-$k$ results.
  • LLM Integration: Groq LLaMA-3.3-70B with a structured prompt restricting answers to retrieved evidence and requiring chunk-ID citations.
  • KPI Extraction: Semi-automated KPI extraction using regex plus manual validation (e.g., revenue, segments, customers, fuel savings, ESG targets).
  • Streamlit App: Company Snapshot tab with KPI cards and overview; Ask-a-Question tab with guided prompts, cited answers, and chunk table.

Results

  • Delivered a functional RAG system that reliably retrieves company, risk, ESG, and shareholder evidence.
  • Provided transparent, cited answers and a two-tab Streamlit dashboard for non-technical users.
  • Documented limitations (OCR quality, manual KPI extraction, chunking strategy, API limits) and recommendations for future upgrades.
